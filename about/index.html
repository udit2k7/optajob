Our Story

Born from the frustration of the Indian job hunt

Every Indian job seeker knows the grind. You apply to hundreds of listings, send generic resumes, never hear back, and have no idea if anyone even saw your application. You waste hours tailoring each resume manually — or you don't, and the ATS filters you out instantly.

After watching friends and family go through this repeatedly, OptaJob was built with one mission: make the entire job search process — finding jobs, tailoring your application, reaching the actual decision-maker — as fast and intelligent as possible.

What started as a personal project became an app designed for every job seeker in India — freshers, experienced professionals, and everyone in between.
