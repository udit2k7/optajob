3. How We Use Your Data

  • Account Management: Your name and email are used to create and manage your account, authenticate you, and communicate with you about your account.
  • Email Verification: We send a 6-digit code to your email address to verify your account during registration and password resets. These codes expire within 24 hours.
  • Job Matching: Your resume text is processed locally on your device to calculate a match percentage against job listings. This processing happens on-device and does not involve sending your resume to our servers.
  • AI Resume Tailoring: When you tap "Tailor Resume" for a specific job, the relevant portion of your resume and the job description are sent to the AI API you have configured (Gemini/OpenAI) via your own API key. We never see or log this content.
  • Contact Finding: When you use the contact finder, only the company domain name is sent to the vendor API you have configured. Your personal information is not included.
  • Credit System: We track credit usage to fairly enforce daily limits and ad credit grants.
  • Improving the App: Anonymised, aggregated analytics data helps us understand which features are used and where the app can be improved. This data cannot be linked to any individual.
  • Crash Reporting: Crash reports help us identify and fix technical issues quickly.
  • Customer Support: Your name and email allow us to respond to support requests you initiate.
  • Legal Compliance: We may process or retain data as required by applicable Indian laws.

We do not use your data for automated decision-making, profiling for employment purposes, or any purpose other than providing and improving the OptaJob service.

5. Data Storage & Security

Local device storage: Your resume content, saved jobs, application history, API keys, and personal preferences are stored exclusively on your Android device using secure local storage (AsyncStorage/SQLite with device-level encryption).

Server storage: Your account data (name, email, hashed password, credit balance, plan type) is stored on Supabase servers hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS) in a secure region.

Security measures we implement:

  • All data in transit is encrypted with TLS 1.2 or higher (HTTPS)
  • Passwords are hashed using SHA-256 with a unique cryptographic salt — never stored in plain text
  • Database access is restricted to authenticated application connections only
  • API keys you enter are stored locally and never transmitted to our servers
  • Admin accounts are protected with separate strong credential requirements

⚠️ While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong unique password and to notify us immediately at support@optajob.com if you suspect unauthorised access to your account.

6. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: We retain your account data for as long as your account remains active.
  • Account deletion: When you delete your account, your name, email, and account data are permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days. Local device data is removed immediately when you uninstall the app.
  • Verification codes: Email verification codes expire after 24 hours and are deleted after use.
  • Crash reports: Firebase Crashlytics retains crash data for 90 days by default.
  • Analytics: Anonymised, aggregated analytics may be retained indefinitely as they cannot identify any individual.
  • Support communications: Emails and support messages are retained for up to 2 years to assist with ongoing or future support needs.

7. Your Rights & How to Exercise Them

As a user of OptaJob, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you. Email us at support@optajob.com with the subject "Data Access Request". We will respond within 30 days.
  • Right to Correction: Update your name and phone number directly in the app under Profile. For email corrections, contact us.
  • Right to Deletion ("Right to be Forgotten"): Delete your account and all associated data: App → Profile → Settings → Delete Account. We will delete your server-side data within 30 days. Local device data is removed on uninstall.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format (JSON). Email us at support@optajob.com with the subject "Data Portability Request".
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You may request we stop processing your data while keeping your account. Email us to discuss.
  • Right to Object to Ads: You may opt out of personalised advertising via: Android Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID (Android 12+) or Opt out of Ads Personalisation (Android 11 and below).
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: If you believe we have mishandled your data, you may contact our Grievance Officer (see Section 12) or file a complaint with the relevant Indian authority.

9. Advertising & AdMob

OptaJob uses Google AdMob to display rewarded video advertisements. Watching an ad is optional and voluntary — users choose to watch ads to earn additional credits.

What AdMob collects:

  • Android Advertising ID (AAID) — a resettable device identifier
  • General device information (OS version, screen size, language)
  • General location (country-level, derived from IP address)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, completions)

How to control ad personalisation:

  • Android 12+: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Delete advertising ID
  • Android 11 and below: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation
  • If you opt out, you will still see ads but they will not be personalised to your interests

AdMob is subject to Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob's Data Practices.
