Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about OptaJob.

Is OptaJob free to use?

Yes. The app operates on an AI Credit Economy. Every user starts with 5 free credits. You can earn infinite free credits by watching short sponsored ads, or purchase premium fiat packs to skip the ads.

How does the app protect my resume data?

OptaJob utilizes a "Bring Your Own Key" (BYOK) architecture. Your Gemini and Groq API keys are encrypted securely to your phone's physical hardware. We do not log your resume text on our central servers.

Is OptaJob only for jobs in India?

Currently, the AI algorithms and market analytics are hyper-tuned for the Indian corporate sector. However, our aggressive roadmap includes imminent launches for the US, Middle East, Australia, and the UK.

How do I buy credits online?

Our secure web-based payment portal is currently under construction. Once live, you will be able to log in to optajob.com to manage your wallet and purchase fiat packs that instantly sync to your mobile app.
