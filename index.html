✦ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is OptaJob really free to start?+
Yes, completely free. You get 3 credits every day for your first 14 days — no credit card, no sign-up fee. Each credit gives you full AI features for one job (resume tailoring + HR contact + cover letter). You can also earn more credits for free by watching short 30-second ads.
Are these real jobs or AI-generated ones?+
Real jobs only. OptaJob connects to professional job APIs (JSearch, Adzuna, and others) that pull live listings from LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, and more. Every "Apply Now" link opens the real job application page at the company's website. We never show AI-invented job listings.
How accurate is the HR contact finder?+
OptaJob uses 9 professional email finder vendors including Hunter.io, Apollo.io, Snov.io, Skrapp.io, and more. Every result shows a confidence level — HIGH (verified by the vendor), MEDIUM (pattern match), or LOW (AI estimate). We always recommend verifying before sending a cold email.
Is my resume data safe?+
Your resume is stored securely on your device. It is only processed when you explicitly request AI tailoring for a specific job. We do not sell your data, share it with employers without your action, or use it for training AI models. See our full Privacy Policy for details.
Which Android versions are supported?+
OptaJob works on Android 8.0 (Oreo) and above. It is fully tested and optimised for Android 14, 15, and 16. We recommend keeping your Android version updated for the best experience.
Will OptaJob be on iOS?+
We are launching on Android first and will consider iOS based on demand. If you want an iOS version, use the contact form — enough interest and we will prioritise it.
