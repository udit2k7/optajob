Quick Answers

How do I delete my account?+
Open the app → Profile tab → scroll to bottom → Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data within 30 days.
I'm not receiving my verification email+
Check your spam/junk folder first. If it's not there, use the "Resend Code" button in the app. Still not working? Email us with your registered email address.
My credits disappeared — what happened?+
Daily free credits reset every 24 hours. Ad credits don't expire for 3 days. If you believe there's an error, email us your registered email address and we'll investigate.
Can I request a refund?+
For paid subscriptions, we offer a 7-day refund if the app did not work as described. Email us within 7 days of purchase with your order details.
I found a bug — how do I report it?+
Use the contact form on this page or email optajob.sup@gmail.com. Include: your Android version, what you were doing, and what happened. Screenshots are very helpful.
