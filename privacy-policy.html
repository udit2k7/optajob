Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

OptaJob ("we", "our", "us") collects basic account information (Email Address) via Firebase Authentication to synchronize your saved jobs and portfolio vault across devices.

2. AI API Keys & On-Device Processing

OptaJob utilizes a Bring Your Own Key (BYOK) model. Your personal API keys (Google Gemini, Groq) are encrypted and stored strictly on your local device hardware. We do not transmit, log, or store your personal AI keys on our servers. All resume parsing and AI generation occurs via direct client-to-provider secure connections.

3. Third-Party Advertising & Cookies

To keep the platform accessible, we utilize third-party vendors, including Google AdMob. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this app or other websites. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

4. Data Security & Isolation

Your portfolio data (Cover Letters, Tailored Resumes) is isolated using strict Firestore security rules. No other user or unauthorized entity can read or access your career documents.

5. Contact

For data deletion requests or privacy inquiries, contact: support@optajob.com.
